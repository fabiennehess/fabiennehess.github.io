ABOUT EXHIBITIONS {% comment %} ATLAS {% endcomment%}

In my work, I use fabric to explore how we see and remember images. I try, whenever possible, to resist hierarchical structures that control access to images and to work with images from the bottom of the pile. I also unravel fabric to examine the ways in which people relate to one another and to talk about loss of matter and memory.
